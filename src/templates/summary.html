ABOUT + DISCLAIMER

We were interested in what the individual seat betting markets were predicting for the 2016 Australian election but couldn’t find a site with an easy summary. So, Geoff made one. Robbie looked over his shoulder, not doing much.

The predicted seats are taken from the Sportsbet odds for individual seats every day at 4am AEST. We assign a win to the candidate with the lowest odds for each seat. That’s all. Nothing more complex than that.

In 2013, seat betting markets over predicted the number of Labor losses. Reasons for this can be found on election lab. Basically, don’t take the result as gospel. Like us though, you might find it another interesting data point in the lead-up to July 2.

Sometimes seats disappear from Sportsbet, so there may be fewer than 150 seats shown some days.

Happy polling.
